What We Do

Personalized Funko-Style Figs

Forget boring gifts. We turn your favorite humans into custom "Funko-style" vinyl figures. Send us photos, and we'll sculpt a ridiculously cute, big-headed caricature that captures their vibe perfectly.

Lithophane Creation

Magic happens when the lights go down. We take your digital photos and 3D print them into textured porcelain-like panes. They look like nothing special until you put a light behind them—bam!

Custom 3D Model Printing

Got an STL file burning a hole in your hard drive? We'll bring it into the real world. Whether it's a cosplay prop, a prototype for your startup, or just a cool meme you found online, we print it with precision.
